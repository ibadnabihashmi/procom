Our registration

Valuing our Participant’s time and energy we have reduced our Registration Process to Three simple steps:
STEP 1: Online Data Submission
 Log onto our website, click on your desired competition and enter all the necessary information. This step is to record your team data into our system.
STEP 2: Registration Ticket and Fee Submission
 Get in contact with your University Ambassador (Ambassadors details are on our website), pay him/her the registration fee and Collect your Registration Ticket. This Ticket is the proof of your fee submission, you are strictly advised to keep this ticket safe and bring it on the event days.
STEP 3: Confirmation by Procom Team
 As soon as you submit the fee to Ambassadors, you will receive a confirmation call/sms from Procom team of the completion of registration.

Ambassador

Become an ambassador

Student ambassadorship will provide an opportunity for students to act as liaisons between FAST-NUCES and their respective universities. So avail the chance of representing your university and hold the leadership responsibility. The core duty of the ambassador will be to keep hold of all the registration fees of his university members and to submit it to us through easypaisa. The ease of this ambassadorship program is to make us all work together as a collaborative team in order to make this event reach the heights of success so come and work together with us as we are the change bringers of this nation.

    The ambassadors behold following duties as per their responsibility:

  • Distribute PROCOM promotional Material in their respective Universities.
  • Assist PROCOM team when they visit their University for promotion.
  • Manage registrations, Collect Registration fee and distribute registrations tickets.
  • Act as a bridge between the participants from their university and PROCOM team.

    Moreover, the perks of holding the position of ambassadorship behold:

  • Free registration on the basis of getting 15 teams for any one specific competition.
  • Ambassadorship awards on the basis of how well the teams of their respective university perform.
  • Free social events tickets and much more which will thrill you up so don’t miss the chance!
